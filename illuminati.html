History

The Illuminati (Bavarian Illuminati) was a secret organization founded in Ingolstadt, Germany on May 1, 1776 by Adam Weishaupt and Baron Adolph von Knigge. The Illuminati consisted of Masons and former Masons. The organization opposed the Roman Catholic Church’s power over science and philosophy. The members sought to free themselves from the church and restrictions of the government. Apparently, the “secret” group did not stay secret for long. After many people gossiped about the mysterious group, the Bavarian government banned the Illuminati as well as the Freemasons.
